Pizza

An Italian staple with an American twist

The Recipe

    What you'll need
  1. 1lb of ground beef
  2. 2lb of mozzarella cheese
  3. 1 red onion sliced
  4. 1 16oz can of pizza sauce
  5. 1 16oz can of chrushed tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Step 1 For sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavors to develop while making crust. Freeze remaining paste.
  2. Step 2 For crusts: Combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 minute). Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed.
  3. Step 3 Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed. (If using RapidRiseÂ® Yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)
  4. Step 4 Divide dough in half. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.
  5. Step 5 For pizzas: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Top crusts with sauce, pepperoni and cheese.
  6. Step 6 Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
